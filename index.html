Accessibility First

Built with WCAG 2.2 AA compliance and tested by the disabled community.

  • Screen Reader Optimized: Full VoiceOver and TalkBack support with semantic navigation
  • High Contrast Mode: Enhanced visibility for low vision users
  • Large Text Support: Scalable typography that adapts to user preferences
  • Voice Control: Complete voice navigation and control capabilities
  • Reduced Motion: Respects user preferences for motion sensitivity
  • Color Accessible: WCAG compliant contrast ratios throughout

Our Mission

AccessLink LGBTQ+ was created by and for the disabled LGBTQ+ community. We believe that accessibility and inclusivity should never be an afterthought.

Our team understands the unique challenges faced when you need both accessibility accommodations and LGBTQ+ affirming spaces. We've experienced the frustration of finding a business that's accessible but not welcoming, or inclusive but not accessible.
